The chassis is the backbone of a car, providing structural support and ensuring safety, durability, and performance.

- In Proton X50 and Proton X70, the chassis is designed using advanced materials like high-strength steel and aluminium alloys.
- These materials are chosen for their strength, lightweight properties, and corrosion resistance.
- The chassis ensures the car can handle forces like acceleration, braking, and cornering while protecting passengers during collisions.

Molecular Structure:
- Steel: Body-Centered Cubic (BCC) for strength.
- Aluminium: Face-Centered Cubic (FCC) for lightweight and corrosion resistance.

Fun Fact: The chassis is like the skeleton of the car, holding everything together and making it strong yet flexible!
